About Us

Home > About Us


CargoBros Logistics Limited is registered and organized in accordance to the corporate laws of the Republic of Kenya. The services offered include; freight forwarding, warehousing, collateral management, airfreight, shipping, and Packing & Removals and multi modal transport.

Our History

CARGOBROS LOGISTICS LTD was formed in 2009 by likeminded individuals who seek cost effective logistical solutions. We pride ourselves in only providing the highest level of service and thus ensuring customer satisfaction. We believe that this will propel us in the market as one of the leading logistics solutions company in Kenya.

The operations of the company include ships agency, freight forwarding and trading activities.

The company has an intuitive understanding of the import tax realm as well as information technology industry. We have harnessed this raw intuition by formalizing our thinking into industry leading processes that can be utilized to make your business solutions a dream.

The company strives to be a leader in providing value-added logistical solutions to our customers by creating a successful partnership with them throughout the business process. Our pledge is to establish lasting relationships with our clients and partners by exceeding their expectations and gaining their trust through exceptional performance and most importantly, through integrity
